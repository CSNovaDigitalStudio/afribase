Our background

Born in Polokwane. Built for African industry.

AfriBase Lubes was conceived in 2023 as a locally driven lubricant business focused on making quality lubrication and ancillary products more accessible across South Africa and the wider region.

Our journey

From a focused start to a scalable industrial platform.

Butao Technical Services and General Projects, trading as AfriBase Lubes, operates through a contract-manufacturing and private-labelling model supported by strategic suppliers and technical partners. The company is positioned in Polokwane for central distribution across Limpopo and beyond.

Our customer focus includes fuel stations, automotive workshops, bakeries, dairy and meat processors, foundries, asphalt businesses, manufacturers, logistics operators and agricultural users.
