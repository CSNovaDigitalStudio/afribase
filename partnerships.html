Grow with us

Flexible partnership models. Shared market opportunity.

AfriBase Lubes offers distribution, private-label, bulk-manufacturing and toll-manufacturing options designed to accelerate market entry and create lasting value.

Strategic options

Choose the operating model that fits your business.

Each option can be structured through a formal agreement with commercial terms tailored to the relationship.

01

AfriBase Distributorship

We manufacture, pack and deliver AfriBase-branded products to your warehouse as required, supported by branded labels, data sheets and promotional material.

02

Private / In-house Brand

Your selected brand name appears on the products while AfriBase handles manufacturing and packaging behind the scenes.

03

Bulk Manufacturing

We manufacture products in bulk for delivery to your premises, allowing your organisation to manage packaging and branding.

04

Toll Manufacturing

We manufacture and, where required, package products using raw materials supplied by the distributor, while branding remains the distributor’s responsibility.
